Cost Reduction through Infrastructure Consolidation and Automation in a collaborative social media platform

Thursday, March 09, 2023

About Client

A collaborative social media product that faced performance and cost issues due to its complex infrastructure. The company deployed its application as containers on Amazon Web Services (AWS) using Amazon Elastic Container Service (ECS), and the database was in Amazon Relational Database Service (RDS). However, the application and database were located in two separate AWS regions, leading to latencies and distrust in the deployment process. The cost of maintaining full infrastructure in both regions was high, and the company needed to reduce its cost spending.

Objective

The objective of the case study is to highlight the steps taken by a collaborative social media platform to reduce the monthly recurring cost of its infrastructure and improve the reliability of its deployment process.

Solution

Company approached the problem by consolidating its infrastructure and automating its deployment process. The company adopted the following steps to achieve its objective:

  • Migration to Kubernetes: Company moved its application to a single self-managed Kubernetes cluster in a single AWS region. This helped the company to simplify its infrastructure and reduce the ongoing maintenance.
  • Consolidation of Environments: Company consolidated the two RDS servers into one and moved both the Elastic File System (EFS) into one region. This reduced the complexity of the infrastructure and allowed the company to focus on its core business.
  • Implementation of CI/CD: Company implemented a Continuous Integration and Continuous Deployment (CI/CD) pipeline to automate its deployment process. This ensured that the deployment was reliable and consistent, leading to a reduction in downtime and maintenance costs.
  • Staging and Production Separation: Company separated the staging and production environments with a namespace in the same Kubernetes cluster and database in the same database server. This ensured that the deployment process was secure and allowed the company to test its application in a controlled environment before deploying it to production.

Technologies

Results

  • The implementation of the above steps resulted in the following benefits for Company:
  • Reduced Monthly Recurring Cost: Company reduced its monthly recurring cost of infrastructure by approximately 70%, leading to significant cost savings.
  • Improved Reliability: The implementation of CI/CD and the migration to Kubernetes ensured that the deployment process was reliable and consistent, leading to a reduction in downtime and maintenance costs.
  • Simplified Infrastructure: The consolidation of environments and the migration to Kubernetes allowed the company to simplify its infrastructure and focus on its core business.
